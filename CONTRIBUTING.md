# Contributing to Awesome DaVinci Resolve

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this curated list of DaVinci Resolve resources!

## How to Submit a Resource

The easiest way to suggest a resource is through our submission form:

👉 **[Submit a Resource]([Google Form link coming soon])**

Fill out the form with details about the tool, plugin, or resource you'd like to add. Submissions are reviewed by the maintainers and added on a regular basis.

### What to Include

When suggesting a resource, please provide:

- **Tool/Resource Name** — Clear, descriptive name
- **Category** — Which section it belongs to (AI & Automation, Audio Plugins, DCTLs, OFX/VFX, Templates/Macros/Scripts, Workflow & Integration)
- **Description** — Brief explanation of what the resource does
- **Website URL** — Direct link to the resource
- **Cost** — Free, paid, freemium, etc.
- **Developer/Creator** — Who makes it (if known)

## Quality Standards

This list follows the [Awesome Manifesto](https://github.com/sindresorhus/awesome/blob/main/awesome.md) standards:

- **High Quality** — Resources must provide genuine value to DaVinci Resolve users
- **Active/Maintained** — Prefer resources that are actively maintained
- **Accessible** — Resources should be publicly accessible
- **No Piracy** — No links to pirated or illegally distributed content

## Review Process

1. You submit a resource via the form
2. Maintainers review the submission for quality and fit
3. Approved resources are added to the list in the next update

## What We Don't Accept

- Duplicate entries (check the list first!)
- Low quality or abandoned resources
- Excessive self-promotion
- Content unrelated to DaVinci Resolve
